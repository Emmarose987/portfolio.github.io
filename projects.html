CTA Transit Ridership vs Chicago Crime

For this project, my team and I looked to answer the question, "do crime rates in Chicago affect CTA ridership?" We sourced two datasets from API's on the CTA website and The Chicago Data Portel. We merged and cleaned the datasets using Pandas, disgarding none-violent crimes and what we determined was unrelated information, and all CTA locations except the train, platform, and station. We plotted the data against each other using Matplotlib, and found that on the days with the highest ridership, crime was also highest. This reflected the same for days of lowest ridership and crime. However, after performing a statitical analysis, we found there was no true correlation between the data. Coming to the conclusion that rate of crime does not affect CTA ridership.
